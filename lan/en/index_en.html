What is Wellness Tourism?

Wellness is a combination of 'well-being', 'happiness' and 'fitness'. Wellness
tourism differs from medical tourism, which needs medical intervention. It refers to
tourism to harmonize mental, social, and physical health by experiencing hot
springs, meditation, yoga, and healthy food throughout the travel.
