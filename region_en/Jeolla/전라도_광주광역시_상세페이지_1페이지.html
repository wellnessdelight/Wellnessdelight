#beauty/spa
Jinan Red Ginseng Spa
Jinan Red Ginseng Spa introduced the first the program of red ginseng herbal medicine and ‘Yin-Yang Five-Element’ in Korea. With the nation's largest health spa facilities and high-quality services, this place consists of a destination spa and a public spa. The destination spa consists of a private space where you can receive various treatments. Unlike conventional public spas in Korea, this spa operates a systematic program to pursue true health and relaxation in the body and mind. In addition, This place provides accommodation facilities, and provides accommodation, food, spa, and auxiliary facilities suitable for various group activities.
Homepage : http://www.redginsengspa.co.kr/
Address : 16-10, Oesayang-gil, Jinan-eup, Jinan-gun, Jeollabuk-do
Tel : 063-433-0396

Wanju Andeok Healing Experience Village
Wanju Andeok Village is operating an experience and health class with the concept of a health healing experience village. In connection with Indeok Village Oriental Medicine Clinic, This place operate health experience programs and health healing classes based on professional knowledge. It is also a village that contributes to the creation of jobs for residents by encouraging the active participation of the residents of Andeok Village. If you go to this site, you can get more information to enjoy your trip.

Ddeularae Guest House & Cafe
This is a guest house located in Namwon, and offers accommodations such as cafes, ondol rooms, and dormitory where you can drink traditional tea. You can also relax by drinking traditional tea at a cafe.
