Unbong Jirisan Herb Valley
It is a good natural experience attraction for families, which operates electric shuttle buses and sky rails. Both inside and outside the botanical garden, you can enjoy different natural scenery from season to season in Herb Science Park. Check out the various experience programs, such as making herb diy.

Village Full of Oxygen and Happiness
You can refresh while breathing fresh air walking through the Chukryeong forest. It is a space realizing well-being and happiness through various forest-related programs and various experiences that can only be experienced here. There are Chukryeong Forest Experience School for Students, Rural Experience School, and Chukryeong Amusement School, which are special programs for the village. It offers a new tour called studying in the countryside.

Ocean Healing Center
The Marine Healing Center, located in Sinan, provides a healing space using natural salt. The main wellness program include a salt cave healing room, mineral floating therapy, a hot-temperature bath of bay salt, a foot bath and a bath of bay salt, and a mineral beauty experience center. Mineral flotation therapy, in the Dead Sea of Israel, makes our body weightless, relaxes our muscles, and converts to meditative brain waves, providing comfort in our body and mind.

Muan Red Clay Mudflat Land
Muan Red Clay Mudflat Land has both accommodation and camping facilities. There are red clay sauna, convenience store, sports facilities, camping sites, playgrounds for children, and exhibition halls for adults. It is a great wellness attraction for families to visit.
