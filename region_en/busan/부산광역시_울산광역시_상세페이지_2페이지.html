Wanastar
Wanastar provides a forest yoga meditation experience that balances mental and physical stability through communication with nature. The Saturday Dawn Yoga Meditation helps you get out of your nervous daily life and have time to healing your thoughts under the energy of green nature in the middle of the forest far from the city center. The experience includes healthy food prepared as a local specialty after meditation.
Homepage : https://wanastar.modoo.at/

Shinheung Temple Stay
Shinheung Temple Stay offers customized experiential temple stay with programs chosen by the visitors, including a Buddhist service and Balwoo Gongyang. It also provides a rest-type temple stay centered on tradition, relaxing in nature, and healing without any special programs. There are beautiful valleys and seas near the temple, so you can have time to look inside while feeling nature.
Homepage : http://www.sinheungsa.co.kr/
