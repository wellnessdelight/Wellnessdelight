#nature
Art Valley, Pocheon
Pocheon Art Valley is a location where traces of modern industrial heritage and culture and art coexist. This place, where nature and art are harmonized, was an abandoned quarry. Therefore, the revival as a tourist attraction means a lot. It is a unique space where visitors can meet history, ecology, culture and arts, and education in one place. Recommendation to those interested in art and nature. The main attractions here are Cheonju Lake, Sculpture Park, Trail, Observatory, and Stone Culture Exhibition Hall.
Homepage : https://artvalley.pocheon.go.kr/
Adress : Art Valley-ro 234, Sinbuk-myeon, Pocheon-si, Gyeonggi-do
Tel : 1668-1035

Herb Island Herb Healing Center, Pocheon
The Herb Healing Center is a herb center operated by Herb Island(the largest herb farm in Korea and the representative brand of premium herb). It offers impressive health, rest, and beauty programs for all visitors.

Starfield Aquafield, Hanam/Goyang
Aquafield provides an astonishing new experience of sauna and water park. In the ‘Media art room’, you will get fascinated by the 360-degree projection of media art while enjoying the sauna. In addition, a private relaxing room with a high-end relaxing chair gives quiet and special space for a stronger recovery. Inside the water park, there is the nation's longest ‘Infinite Pool’ with a great view of the Han River and Geomdansan Mountain.
Homepage : https://www.aquafield-ssg.co.kr/
Adress : 750 Misa-daero, Hanam-si, Gyeonggi-do(hanam starfield aquafield), 1955 Goyang-daero, Deokyang-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do(goyang starfield aquafield)
Tel : 031-8072-8800(hanam starfield aquafield), 031-5173-4500(goyang starfield aquafield)
