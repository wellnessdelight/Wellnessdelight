Byeokchoji Arboretum
Byeokchoji Arboretum is full of beautiful nature. It's an opportunity to get great pictures of memory in Korea. One thing to remind, there is nothing much to see in winter. But there will be colorful flowers and trees surrounding everywhere by mid-spring.
Homepage : http://www.bcj.co.kr/
Adress : 242 Buheung-ro, Gwangtan-myeon, Paju-si, Gyeonggi-do
Tel : 031-957-2004
#nature
The Garden of Morning Calm
The Garden of Morning Calm, built on Chukryeongsan Mountain in Gapyeong, embraces the natural beauty of Korea, oriental mystique, and the spirit of the Korean people in the garden. Gardens of 20 different themes are designed with artistic senses. It is also known for the filming location of the Korean drama, “Love in the Moonlight”. Take time to relax and try various hands-on activities, which vary by four seasons.
Homepage : https://www.morningcalm.co.kr/html/main.php
Adress : 432 Arboretum-ro, Sangmyeon, Gapyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do
Tel : 1544-6703

Zara Island
Jara Island was built when Cheongpyeong Dam, Korea's first power generation dam, was completed in 1943. Today it is one of the famous tourist attractions in Gapyeong. Singing festivals and camping programs are prepared. Put all the stress behind it and have a rest for your body and mind.
Homepage : http://www.jarasum.net/
Adress : 60 Jarasum-ro, Gapyeong-eup
Tel : 031-8078-8028

Camptong Island
Camp Tong Island is a premium glamping site which is in Gapyeong. Here you can enjoy glamping and water leisure at the same time. It has 28 glamping sites, barbecue places, coffee shops, Infinity Pool, and leisure facilities. As a ‘premium glamping site’, they have art galleries for visitors to enjoy. It offers you a nice comfortable rest in the nature and luxury at the same time.
Homepage : http://www.camptong.com/html/main.asp#none
Adress : 976 Hoban-ro, Cheongpyeong-myeon, Gapyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do
Tel : 031-584-6000
