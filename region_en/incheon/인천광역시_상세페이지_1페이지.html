#nature
Manisan Forest of Healing
Manisan is the highest mountain on Ganghwa Island. Manisan Mountain's Forest of Healing, designated as a 'UNTACT' tour attraction in Korea, has a 1 km short hiking course. While walking along the forest path full of pine and cedar trees, there are shelters ready for the you to take a minute to meditate and enjoy the fresh breeze.
Homepage : http://www.ganghwafocus.com/exp/index.php
Adress : 18, Manisan-ro 675beon-gil, Hwado-myeon, Ganghwa-gun, Incheon
Tel : 070-4101-8308
#oriental_medicine
Yakseaokwon
Yakseokwon provides a special experience with Ganghwa's own Artemisia princeps, also known as Korean wormwood. This traditional thermal therapy will be a new experience for you. It uses hot artemisia princeps to release waste from the body. After the therapy, there is a relaxation time prepared for you to enjoy a cup of tea with Gangwa Island's fascinating view.
Homepage : https://ysc533.modoo.at/?link=2zf7yx5i
Adress : 56-56, Jungang-ro 787beon-gil, Yangdo-myeon, Ganghwa-gun
Tel : 032-937-5338
