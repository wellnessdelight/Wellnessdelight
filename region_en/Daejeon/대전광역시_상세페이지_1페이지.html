Soje-dong Alley
Soje-dong alley, which almost have disappeared due to the indifference, transformed into a new space with interesting experience. Thanks to Ikseon Dada, a company specializing in urban regeneration that successfully developed Ikseon-dong, Soje-dong alley have become a popular place for healing and relaxation. There are many unique restaurants and cafes. Typically, there are Italian restaurant "Pound," which makes pasta or pizza with ingredients produced in Chungcheong-do, "Kwansachon Coffee," where you can taste Korea's first coffee, Yangtangguk, and "Pungyuga," where you can taste tea leaves mixed with fruit syrup, rum, and barcardi at an appropriate ratio. You can enjoy the beauty and relaxation of the tradition at the shops in Soje-dong Alley. In addition, walk along a quiet and narrow alley looking at the murals painted on each wall. You can feel the sense of freedom that this road is your own space.

Jangtae Mountain Natural Recreation Forest
Jangtae Mountain Natural Recreation Forest is well-known for a magnificent view of stretched mountain range rooted in Daedun Mountain, and the largest Metasegua Forest in Korea. The exotic scenery changes its style throughout the four seasons, drawing admiration from visitors. Jangtaesan Natural Recreation Forest opened for free for citizens' convenience of rest (except for accommodation and campsites) makes visitors visit it every day for forest bathing, hiking, and resting. At Jangtae mountain Natural Recreation Forest, you can have an unique experience of walking through Skyway as a forest adventure. In addition, you can go up to the Sky Tower to enjoy the spectacular view of Jangtaesan Mountain. Also check the suspension bridge, which is a specialty of the recreational forest, providing a pleasant experience.
Homepage : https://www.foresttrip.go.kr/indvz/main.do?hmpgId=ID02030106

Tel : 042-583-0094

The Season of Tea
The Season of Tea is a tea cafe with a comfortable atmosphere where you can enjoy seasonal refreshments selected by the tea master. The tea course runs only three days from Thursday to Saturday, and only one team receives reservations by the hour. Which means you can enjoy the food slowly while listening to 1:1 customized explanations in a private space. In addition to tea courses in the tea season, you can also enjoy afternoon tea sets made with different fruits and tea from season to season.
