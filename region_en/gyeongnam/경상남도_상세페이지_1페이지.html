Sancheong Donguibogam Village
Sancheong Donguibogam Village is an oriental medicine experience site where you can enjoy anti-aging healing experience along with nature. They provide different programs, such as thermal courses, oriental medicine courses, and foot spas. They also have accommodations in various themes for you to enjoy. Sancheong Oriental Family Hotel has a nature-friendly lifestyle theme, and Oriental Recreational Forest has a theme of oriental medicine. It makes Sancheong Donguibogam Village a special wellness tour attraction.
Homepage : http://donguibogam-village.sancheong.go.kr/
Adress : 45-6 Donguibogam-ro 555beon-gil, Sancheong-gun, Gyeongsangnam-do
Tel : 055-970-7216

#healing/meditation
Hapcheon Odosan Forest of Healing
Odosan Forest of Healing is the best place to enjoy forest healing. It is known for its impressive view of the pine tree forest. You can experience various healing programs, such as silver yoga, aromatherapy, and lower-body baths that help the recovery of the body from fatigue. Breathing fresh air while walking along the street and sitting in a shelter for meditation will make you relieve stress and feel relaxed.
Homepage : https://www.foresttrip.go.kr/indvz/main.do?hmpgId=ID02030068#
Adress : 208 Odosan Recreational Road, Bongsan-myeon, Hapcheon-gun, Gyeongsangnam-do
Tel : 055-930-3733

Tongyeong Napoli Farm
Napoli Farm offers a new experience of the spa in the nature of the cypress forest. Barefoot experience, forest baths, phytoncide foot baths, and phytoncide skincare are the programs they provide. From those, 'cypress forest barefoot healing' is the most popular program. It enhances the body's immunity by walking barefoot on the path of cypress sawdust. They take good care of the system by cleaning the path at 4 a.m. every day and getting young cypress trees (up to 25 years old) which manage the release of phytoncide the most.
Homepage : http://www.naporyair.com/skin_mw1/
Adress : 765-21 Yeongun-ri, Sanyang-eup, Tongyeong-si, Gyeongsangnam-do
Tel : 055-641-7005
