Geochang Sky Lake
Geochang Sky Lake conducts a program of making customized cosmetics using natural herbal ingredients. Cosmetics are made by using precious herbal ingredients and excluding all the unnecessary chemicals. It helps regenerate skin and strengthen skin barriers. It also uses oriental water extracted by distillation after aging and fermenting high-quality herbal medicines. These cosmetics does not contain preservatives, alcohol, and chemical additives, so it can be used by anyone from children to adults with sensitive skin.
