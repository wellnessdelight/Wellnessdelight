Thera Spa
Thera Spa is a medical spa located in Chaum Clinic. It uses the world's first patented ingredient, skin differentiation stem cells, to improve wrinkles caused by aging and strengthen with moisture. Bio Platinum Treatment is a special therapy provided using a low-frequency device that stretches and relocates fine muscles in the rarely used face to manage facial asymmetry correction effects and V-line contours. You can experience Thera Spa's program of immunization, pain management, slimming, and wellness spa.

#healing/meditation
Hwagyesa Temple
Hwagyesa Temple is located very close to the center of the city, but it has dense forests surrounding it. In the temple stay program, you can relax at the temple for up to 3 nights and 4 days, and experience everything about Buddhism. Participants have agreed that the most impressive time was the Cha Dam, a program where you ask the chief monk a question and hear the answers.
Homepage : http://www.hwagyesa.org
