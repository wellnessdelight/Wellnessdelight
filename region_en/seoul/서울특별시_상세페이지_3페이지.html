#healing/meditation
Bongeunsa Temple
Bongeun Temple offers an opportunity to explore the existence, human relationships, and direction of life. Through a one-night two-day temple stay program, you can experience the life of an attendant. There are different programs of learning the worship of Buddha, making a pilgrimage to the temple, and performing Zen meditation.
Homepage http://temple.bongeunsa.org
Adress : 531 Bongeun-sa-ro, Gangnam-gu, Seoul
Tel : 02-3218-4800
#beauty/spa
Beauty Boulevard
K-beauty is leading the global trend as well as K-pop. Korean cosmetics and makeup methods are attracting attention from people around the world, which led to curiosity about where celebrities visit. Beauty Blvad in Cheongdam-dong is well-known for being managed by Super Junior's Lee teuk and actor Hwang Jung-eum. Try out the relaxing beauty spa form where Korean celebrities get.

#nature
Seoul Bontanic Park
The Seoul Botanical Garden, located in Magok Urban Development Zone, was established to increase the ecological sensitivity of the city. It allows citizens to enjoy plants in their daily lives and enhances the public interest in preserving and restoring urban natural ecosystems.
Homepage : https://botanicpark.seoul.go.kr/front/main.do
Adress : Seoul Botanical Garden, 161, Magok-dong-ro, Gangseo-gu, Seoul
Tel : 02-2104-9716
#oriental_medicine
Seoul K Medi Center
It's a complex cultural space on oriental medicine. Under the theme of traditional Korean medicine, the Seoul K Medi Center offers museum exhibitions, the knowledge of oriental medicine, foot baths, and medicinal food. As it is in Yakryeong-si, Seoul, the largest medicinal herb market in Korea, it is possible to purchase high-quality health food and oriental medicine. This wellness tourist attraction is where you can enjoy culture, the fun of traveling, as well as your health.
Homepage : https://kmedi.ddm.go.kr/
Adress : 26 Yakryeong Jungang-ro, Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul
Tel : 02-969-9241
