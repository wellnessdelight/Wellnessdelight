Nodeul Island Park
Nodeul Island Park is a complex cultural space with a live house, rehearsal studio, Nodeul office, lounge bar, and a bookstore. The Urban Innovation Group has turned an abandoned beach into a wellness attraction. It is now a popular spot for a picnic and a healing shelter.

Melixir
Melixer is Korea's the first brand to introduce 100% vegan cosmetics. It is certified by PETA(the People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals), a global animal rights organization. Melixer runs a "Make a Vegan Bath Bomb" program on Airbnb Trip, where you can study what ingredients are in the cosmetics and make a bath bomb that suits your taste.

Oil Tank Culture Park
Oil Tank Culture Park is an oil reserve base that is a legacy of the industrial era. This place, where they had strictly controlled access and use of the general public for 41 years, has been reborn as an ecological and cultural park through urban regeneration. You can rent an audio guide (Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese) from the information center for further information. Don't forget to check out the cultural events such as exhibitions, performances, and workshops held at the Oil Tank Culture Park.
Homepage : http://parks.seoul.go.kr/culturetank
Adress : 18-11 Seoul Forest 2-gil, Seongdong-gu, Seoul
Tel : 02-376-8410
