Gyeongju East Place Garden
Gyeongju East Place Garden, located in Gyeongju Bomun Tourist Complex, has modernized the first zoo and a botanical garden, called Donggung and Wolji, built in Korea. You can observe various plants and birds at the East Place Garden and Bird Park, which has plants that help human health and recovery. You also have a chance to meet various animals at the ecological experience center.
Homepage : http://gyeongjuepg.kr/
Address : 74-14 Bomun-ro, Gyeongju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do
Tel : 054-779-8725

The National Healing Forest of Daeun Mountain
The National Healing Forest of Daeun Mountain has refreshing valley, oriental oak tree, cypress trees, and various forest healing programs operated in the forest road. Forest healing program utilizes various environmental elements of the forest. It is an activity that increases the body's immunity and restores physical and mental health, and consists of 10 programs that can be enjoyed by both men and women of all ages, from "Five Senses Playground in the Forest" to "detox clinic of forest."
Homepage : https://www.fowi.or.kr/user/contents/contentsView.do?cntntsId=161
Address : 225-92, Daeunsangdae-gil, Onyang-eup, Ulju-gun, Ulsan
Tel : 052-255-9800

Baekdudaegan National Arboretum
The National Baekdudaegan Arboretum is Asia's largest and the world's second-largest arboretum, about the size of six soccer fields put together. You can observe a variety of rare plants and ecological gardens. They also have a seed vault to preserve disappearing plants. In addition, the tiger forest nearby on Mt. Baekdu is a must-see attraction.
Homepage : https://www.bdna.or.kr/main/main.do
Address : 1501 Chunyang-ro, Chunyang-myeon, Bonghwa-gun, Gyeongsangbuk-do
Tel : 054-679-1000
