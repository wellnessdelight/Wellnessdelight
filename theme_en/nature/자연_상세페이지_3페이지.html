National Center for Forest Therapy
National Center for Forest Therapy, located in Sobaeksan Mountain, is a space where you can experience the healing effect of the forest. It is equipped with a healing forest path, a water treatment center, and accommodation facilities. Accommodation facilities are divided into Yeongju Juchi Village and Yecheon Munpil Village, one for short-term resident stay and the other for long-term resident stay. In the Health Promotion Center, you can experience healing programs in a dry healing room and an aqua healing room. You can also get under-water exercise and water pressure massage. Get a great healing time for your body and mind.
Homepage : https://daslim.fowi.or.kr/
Address : 209, Therapy-ro, Bonghyeon-myeon, Yeongju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do
Tel : 054-639-3400

The Green Lab
Green Lab, designed to enjoy personal time, is operated with a pre-booking system called 'Enjoying my own Seoul forest'. You can check it out in Naver reservations. They provide a rattan basket containing refreshments, wildflowers, papers, envelopes, writing utensils, and books as a welcome gift. At Green Lab, you get to take a close look at yourself, write a diary, read a book and concentrate. Enjoy your relaxing time on an open rooftop.
Homepage : http://www.instagram.com/greenlab_seoulforest
Adress : 18-11 Seoul Forest 2-gil, Seongdong-gu, Seoul, Republic of Korea
Tel : 02-499-5833

Nature Park
Nature Park is a 100% real natural communion-type ecological zoo. It consists of a large-scale glass house zoo and an outdoor zoo. You can see more than 300 animals, 350 species of trees, and 100 different types of flowers. There are programs to interact with animals and other fun programs such as kayaking and horseback riding.
Homepage : http://www.spavalley.co.kr/naturepark/
Address : 891, Gachang-ro, Gachang-myeon, Dalseong-gun, Daegu
Tel : 1688-8511

Nodeul Island Park
Nodeul Island Park is a complex cultural space with a live house, rehearsal studio, Nodeul office, lounge bar, and a bookstore. The Urban Innovation Group has turned an abandoned beach into a wellness attraction. It is now a popular spot for a picnic and a healing shelter.
Homepage : http://www.nodeul.org
Adress : 445, Yangnyeong-ro, Yongsan-gu, Seoul
Tel : 02-749-4500
