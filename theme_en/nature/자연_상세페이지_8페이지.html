Zara Island
Jara Island was built when Cheongpyeong Dam, Korea's first power generation dam, was completed in 1943. Today it is one of the famous tourist attractions in Gapyeong. Singing festivals and camping programs are prepared. Put all the stress behind it and have a rest for your body and mind.
Homepage : http://www.jarasum.net/
Adress : 60 Jarasum-ro, Gapyeong-eup
Tel : 031-8078-8028

Jangtae Mountain Natural Recreation Forest
Jangtae Mountain Natural Recreation Forest is well-known for a magnificent view of stretched mountain range rooted in Daedun Mountain, and the largest Metasegua Forest in Korea. The exotic scenery changes its style throughout the four seasons, drawing admiration from visitors. Jangtaesan Natural Recreation Forest opened for free for citizens' convenience of rest (except for accommodation and campsites) makes visitors visit it every day for forest bathing, hiking, and resting. At Jangtae mountain Natural Recreation Forest, you can have an unique experience of walking through Skyway as a forest adventure. In addition, you can go up to the Sky Tower to enjoy the spectacular view of Jangtaesan Mountain. Also check the suspension bridge, which is a specialty of the recreational forest, providing a pleasant experience.
Homepage : https://www.foresttrip.go.kr/indvz/main.do?hmpgId=ID02030106
Address : 461, Jangan-ro, Seo-gu, Daejeon
Tel : 042-583-0094

Jinan Wild Herb Town
Jinan wild herb Town is open free of charge even at night, so you can enjoy a walk freely. As a paid experience program, they offer programs such as making products related to wild herbs, a tour of natural scenery (Sanyakcho Road) and making natural handkerchiefs.

Camptong Island
Camp Tong Island is a premium glamping site which is in Gapyeong. Here you can enjoy glamping and water leisure at the same time. It has 28 glamping sites, barbecue places, coffee shops, Infinity Pool, and leisure facilities. As a ‘premium glamping site’, they have art galleries for visitors to enjoy. It offers you a nice comfortable rest in the nature and luxury at the same time.
Homepage : http://www.camptong.com/html/main.asp#none
Adress : 976 Hoban-ro, Cheongpyeong-myeon, Gapyeong-gun, Gyeonggi-do
Tel : 031-584-6000
