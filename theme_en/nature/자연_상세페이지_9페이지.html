Tongyeong Napoli Farm
Napoli Farm offers a new experience of the spa in the nature of the cypress forest. Barefoot experience, forest baths, phytoncide foot baths, and phytoncide skincare are the programs they provide. From those, 'cypress forest barefoot healing' is the most popular program. It enhances the body's immunity by walking barefoot on the path of cypress sawdust. They take good care of the system by cleaning the path at 4 a.m. every day and getting young cypress trees (up to 25 years old) which manage the release of phytoncide the most.
Homepage : http://www.naporyair.com/skin_mw1/
Adress : 765-21 Yeongun-ri, Sanyang-eup, Tongyeong-si, Gyeongsangnam-do
Tel : 055-641-7005

Forest Resom
Forest Resom, where you can enjoy the beautiful four seasons of dense primeval forests, is an eco-friendly resort developed in a way that does not damage nature. Forest Resources is designed to preserve natural terrain and vegetation and use eco-friendly materials to relax with nature. Therefore, you can't find electric wires and cars in the complex. You can only meet beautiful wildflowers, squirrels, and blue squirrels all year round. This resort also offers exhibitions at the Wellness Studio ‘Muyougonggan’ located in the facility, or night-time shooting of constellations at the cozy Igloo Dome.
Homepage : https://www.resom.co.kr/forest/main/main.asp
Address : 365, Geumbong-ro, Baegun-myeon, Jecheon-si, Chungcheongbuk-do
Tel : 1600-0060

Art Valley, Pocheon
Pocheon Art Valley is a location where traces of modern industrial heritage and culture and art coexist. This place, where nature and art are harmonized, was an abandoned quarry. Therefore, the revival as a tourist attraction means a lot. It is a unique space where visitors can meet history, ecology, culture and arts, and education in one place. Recommendation to those interested in art and nature. The main attractions here are Cheonju Lake, Sculpture Park, Trail, Observatory, and Stone Culture Exhibition Hall.
Homepage : https://artvalley.pocheon.go.kr/
Adress : Art Valley-ro 234, Sinbuk-myeon, Pocheon-si, Gyeonggi-do
Tel : 1668-1035

Polgae Cooperative Association
The Polgae Cooperative is a place made up of returnees. It operates an organic blueberry farm and a tangerine farm, and beekeeping bees with Hallasan wildflowers as a wheat source. There is a farming experience (blueberry picking, tangerine picking) at the farm. They often visit with their children as a family unit.
Homepage : http://polgae.com/
