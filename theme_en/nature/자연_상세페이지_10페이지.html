Haeden Museum of Art
Haden Museum is a unique art museum surrounded by the forest. The harmony of nature and modern architecture seems like it's a work of art. You can find works of famous contemporary artists and also enjoy outdoor garden.
homepage : http://www.haedenmuseum.com
Adress : 37-41, Jeondeungsa-ro, Gilsang-myeon, Ganghwa-gun, Incheon
tel : 032-937-6911(6912)

Muan Red Clay Mudflat Land
Muan Red Clay Mudflat Land has both accommodation and camping facilities. There are red clay sauna, convenience store, sports facilities, camping sites, playgrounds for children, and exhibition halls for adults. It is a great wellness attraction for families to visit.
Homepage : https://www.muan.go.kr/getbol
Address : 34, Mansong-ro, Haeje-myeon, Muan-gun, Jeollanam-do
Tel : 061-450-5636
