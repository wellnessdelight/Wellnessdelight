Sancheong Donguibogam Village
Sancheong Donguibogam Village is an oriental medicine experience site where you can enjoy anti-aging healing experience along with nature. They provide different programs, such as thermal courses, oriental medicine courses, and foot spas. They also have accommodations in various themes for you to enjoy. Sancheong Oriental Family Hotel has a nature-friendly lifestyle theme, and Oriental Recreational Forest has a theme of oriental medicine. It makes Sancheong Donguibogam Village a special wellness tour attraction.

Seoul K Medi Center
It's a complex cultural space on oriental medicine. Under the theme of traditional Korean medicine, the Seoul K Medi Center offers museum exhibitions, the knowledge of oriental medicine, foot baths, and medicinal food. As it is in Yakryeong-si, Seoul, the largest medicinal herb market in Korea, it is possible to purchase high-quality health food and oriental medicine. This wellness tourist attraction is where you can enjoy culture, the fun of traveling, as well as your health.
Homepage : https://kmedi.ddm.go.kr/
Adress : 26 Yakryeong Jungang-ro, Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul
Tel : 02-969-9241

Yakseaokwon
Yakseokwon provides a special experience with Ganghwa's own Artemisia princeps, also known as Korean wormwood. This traditional thermal therapy will be a new experience for you. It uses hot artemisia princeps to release waste from the body. After the therapy, there is a relaxation time prepared for you to enjoy a cup of tea with Gangwa Island's fascinating view.
