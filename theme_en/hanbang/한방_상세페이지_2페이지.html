Lee Mun-won, Oriental Medicine Clinic
Lee Moon-won is an oriental medical clinic that treats hair loss, scalp diseases, and hair problems with oriental medicine. It is characterized by taking care of hair using Cham comb, a traditional comb in Korea. The programs are largely divided into two, a treatment spa that combines treatment and a healing spa that can be used by anyone.

Oriental Medicine Life Science Museum
In Oriental Medicine Life Science Museum, you can learn about our bodies, the history of diseases, the principles, diagnosis, and treatment of oriental medicine through 4D images and various oriental medicine experiences. Located in Jecheon Oriental Medicine Expo Park, Oriental Medicine Life Science Museum is free of charge, and you can experience hop therapy and wellness hop picnic programs using the eco-friendly beer raw material hops grown in Jecheon for the first time in Korea. Hop Therapy/Picnic is a wellness program for young women or lovers interested in hop beauty, spa, and is an easy-to-experience wellness program for beginners.
