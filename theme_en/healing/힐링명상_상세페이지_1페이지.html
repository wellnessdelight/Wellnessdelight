International Seon Center
International Seon Center promotes Korean Buddhism and traditional culture. Here, you can experience domestic Buddhist cultures such as temple stay, temple cuisine, and other learning programs. Among them, temple stay and meditation classes specialized for foreigners are impressive. The center has a unique system dedicated to foreigners, so don't worry about the language. They provide a guide in English at any time, kindly teaching them Buddhist traditions.
Homepage : http://www.seoncenter.or.kr
Adress : 167, Mokdong-ro, Yangcheon-gu, Seoul International Temple Stay Center
Tel : 02-2650-2200

Deep Mountain Ongdalsaem
Under the theme of "Stop for a while," it runs various programs focusing on life meditation that anyone can participate in, such as healing meditation and family meditation. You can heal your body and mind. Deep Mountain Ongdalsaem is a meditation healing center established by Godowon, chairman of the Morning Letter Cultural Foundation, with the support of members. It also offers a variety of life meditation programs for a short rest and refresh. you can relax and meditate freely overnight or for a few days.
Homepage : https://godowoncenter.com/
Address : 201-61, Useong 1-gil, Noeun-myeon, Chungju-si, Chungcheongbuk-do
Tel : 1644-8421

Jogye Order of Korean Buddhism Beomeo Temple Stay
Beomeo Temple Stay provides time to look back and recharge with idyllic atmosphere for those who seek a true happiness trip in peaceful place. Through forest path meditation, early morning buddist worship, making 108 times Seowonju, and early morning meditation, you can Gain inner stability and healing. All participants can choice ‘experience-type Temple Stay' and 'Rest-type Temple Stay' program.
Homepage : http://www.beomeo.kr/templestay/ts01.php
Address : 250, Beomeosa-ro, Geumjeong-gu, Busan
Tel : 051-508-5726
