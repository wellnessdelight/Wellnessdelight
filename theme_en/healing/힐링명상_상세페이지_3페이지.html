Soje-dong Alley
Soje-dong alley, which almost have disappeared due to the indifference, transformed into a new space with interesting experience. Thanks to Ikseon Dada, a company specializing in urban regeneration that successfully developed Ikseon-dong, Soje-dong alley have become a popular place for healing and relaxation. There are many unique restaurants and cafes. Typically, there are Italian restaurant "Pound," which makes pasta or pizza with ingredients produced in Chungcheong-do, "Kwansachon Coffee," where you can taste Korea's first coffee, Yangtangguk, and "Pungyuga," where you can taste tea leaves mixed with fruit syrup, rum, and barcardi at an appropriate ratio. You can enjoy the beauty and relaxation of the tradition at the shops in Soje-dong Alley. In addition, walk along a quiet and narrow alley looking at the murals painted on each wall. You can feel the sense of freedom that this road is your own space.
Homepage : https://korean.visitkorea.or.kr/detail/rem_detail.do?cotid=c557e923-5e10-492b-ab20-6e8edb2dfbc9&con_type=11400
Address : 2, Cheolgap 2-gil, Dong-gu, Daejeon
Tel :042-251-4114

Shinheung Temple Stay
Shinheung Temple Stay offers customized experiential temple stay with programs chosen by the visitors, including a Buddhist service and Balwoo Gongyang. It also provides a rest-type temple stay centered on tradition, relaxing in nature, and healing without any special programs. There are beautiful valleys and seas near the temple, so you can have time to look inside while feeling nature.
Homepage : http://www.sinheungsa.co.kr/
